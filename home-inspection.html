Home Inspections

Price: $300 under 1800 sq. Ft.

Know What You're Buying

During the inspection we look at all aspects of the home to determine what condition each of its features are in. We will then provide you with an accurate and unbiased assessment of the property. Education and experience is our best tool and it is our duty to relay that information to you the client, in a clear and detailed way so that you may make an informed decision.

Full Color Report

The report is made up of 13 to 15 sections, including kitchen, bathrooms, heat/AC, electrical, roof, exterior areas and more. Within these sections there are over 200 individual items which we evaluate thoroughly. Items found in poor condition are photographed and documented with comments at the end of the report.

Definition of a home inspection: Technically exhaustive non-invasive inspection usually performed for new home buyers prior to purchase so they may better understand the current condition of the property.

Our home inspection process starts with a two to four hour visit to the home you are interested in buying or selling. During this time we look at all aspects of the home to determine what condition each of its features are in. If you are present during the inspection we can walk you through and point out any major findings and answer any questions you may have.

The price for a home inspection in the Tampa Bay area starts at $300 for a home under 1800 sq. ft. If the home is larger or you need additional services along with the home inspection call Kevin today!.
